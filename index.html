The Philosophy

Scent is Memory
Made Eternal

We believe fragrance is the most intimate form of self-expression. At LUMIÈRE, every drop is a conversation between the ancient art of perfumery and the unbounded spirit of the modern world.

Our master perfumers travel the globe — from the jasmine fields of Grasse to the oud forests of Cambodia — to source only the rarest, most exceptional raw materials.

Our Story

Born in
Grasse,
Worn
Worldwide

Founded in 1978 by master perfumer Élise Moreau, LUMIÈRE began as a singular vision: to create fragrances that outlive fashion and transcend trend.

Today, three generations later, that obsessive pursuit of olfactory perfection continues — one rare ingredient, one perfect accord, one unforgettable memory at a time.
