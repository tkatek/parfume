Our Story

A Legacy of
Passion
& Purity

Lumière Parfums was born from a passion for timeless elegance and the art of fine perfumery. Each creation is a tribute to tradition, innovation, and the beauty that connects us all.

Founded in 1978 by master perfumer Élise Moreau, three generations later that obsessive pursuit of olfactory perfection continues.
